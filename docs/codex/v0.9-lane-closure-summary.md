## Still Not Approved

This lane closure does not approve:

- public/security-product positioning;
- production identity or enterprise RBAC;
- arbitrary Git execution;
- raw tag names, tag messages, signatures, stable tag-name hashes, raw diffs, blob/file contents,
  checkout, branch/tag mutation, remote fetch, credentials, or shell;
- broader filesystem writes or new write paths;
- future governed tool powers without their own proposal, gate, implementation decision, and review.

## Current Working Rule

Internal xhigh review may unblock continued local-preview development for bounded, read-only lanes
when:

- no critical/high findings remain open;
- focused and full gates pass;
- the tool-surface/no-new-powers gates remain green;
- the change does not alter the product boundary.

GPT 5.5 Pro / Very High or human review remains the break-glass option for public/security-product
claims, major product-boundary decisions, new powerful tool classes, or unresolved high/critical
findings.
